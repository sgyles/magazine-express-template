Better your work out with music

Andy Garcia Date Technology

Picture from: https://www.wareable.com/hearables/under-armour-sport-wireless-heart-rate-review

Wireless Headphones or wireless earphones to hit the gym? Spending a crazy amount just to listen to music while you workout is stupid. It’s just too much for a few features. But how about some wireless headphones that do more than play your favorite song while you get exercise. Under Armour and JBL just recently came together to create the JBL UA Sport Wireless Heart Rate Headphones. Yup. Wireless Heart Rate Headphone. This new tech can record your heart rate at live time. Collecting data and sending it to your electronic device using the Under Armour app to record the amount of calories you burn, your heart BPM, intensity, and the amount of willpower that you have, all through this piece of technology. These wireless headphones cost $199.99 it may seem a lot but you might spend the same amount of money for some Beats headphones that just play music. It is a reasonable amount of money for headphones that do more than music. These were created for anyone to use and easy to work with while you’re at the gym. The app that records all of this information is called UA MapMyRun.app. It’s simple to use and it’s free to download.

Normal headphones or earphones are a thing from the past. The JBL UA headphones allow you to improve and get better at working out by combing technology that blasts your music to your full potential and the necessary information you need to know to keep you fit and healthy.
