3 Smart Hack Productivity Hacks

Sheyla Gyles 6:49 AM Lifestyle

[Digital image]. (n.d.). Retrieved from https://www.pinterest.com/pin/488007309593067089/

1.Pomodoro Technique
Have you ever been in an exam where you are running out of time and suddenly all of your great ideas start flowing away? Maybe when thinking about this question your answer is no because you are a part of the majority of students who work well under pressure. On the other hand, if your answer to this question is yes, that is so me! One of the best ways to drill out this bad habit is to do your work/tests using the Pomodoro technique. This is because it makes all and complete use of ones adrenaline rush. This is done by breaking your work into 25 minute periods with small 5 minute breaks in between.This technique is very useful because it pushes you to work faster and more efficiently within the period of time given, and not only that but you can also essentially work at your own pace.

2. Stay Focused On One Topic
When you are trying to achieve multiple things at one time you may feel as though you are trying to accomplish too many things at one time. Do not do this!, that is not the way to go. Trust me I am a victim of this, but this is especially bad if you are studying. According to NPR.org, the brain is physically not equipped enough to multitask. When you are trying yo do more things at once, what you are really doing instead of studying is overloading your brain with too much information that you brain simply canâ€™t handle. You often realise you have ended the day having accomplished and retained nothing at all.

3. Always Get a Goodnights Sleep!
Although caffeine may help you stay awake during the day, nothing keeps you up better than getting a good night's rest. Getting a good night's rest is imperative to the function of the body. This goes a very long way to help you stay more productive throughout the duration of the entire day. In order to for your body to be completely awake and functioning properly, the average human needs approximately 7-10 hours of sleep. Try this and not only will you be more productive during the day but you will also feel great!
